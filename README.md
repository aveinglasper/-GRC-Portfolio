# GRC Portfolio — Avein Glasper

Cybersecurity Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) portfolio developed as applied practice during B.S. Cybersecurity Management & Policy studies at the University of Maryland Global Campus (UMGC).

**Frameworks Referenced:** NIST SP 800-30, NIST CSF 2.0, NIST SP 800-18, SANS policy templates

---

## Portfolio Contents

### 1. Risk Register — NIST SP 800-30
15-risk enterprise assessment for fictional organization Meridian Tech Solutions. Includes auto-calculated risk scoring dashboard, color-coded risk levels (Critical/High/Medium/Low), treatment plans, and methodology documentation tab.

### 2. Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) — NIST CSF 2.0
Formal organizational security policy covering authorized use, prohibited activities, remote access, password requirements, incident reporting, and user acknowledgment. Aligned to NIST CSF 2.0 Govern and Protect functions.

### 3. GRC Framework Comparison — In progress
Written analysis comparing NIST RMF, ISO 27001, and SOC 2 across structure, scope, and applicability.
